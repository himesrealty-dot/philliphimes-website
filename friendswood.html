Local Knowledge

Living in Friendswood, TX

Friendswood is one of Texas's most consistently loved communities — a small town that has somehow stayed a small town despite being surrounded by one of America's largest metro areas. Founded by Quakers in 1895, it carries a quiet dignity and community-first culture that residents protect fiercely.

The school district is the crown jewel. Friendswood ISD punches well above its size — producing state championship athletic programs alongside academic excellence. For families prioritizing education, Friendswood is a top-five destination in the entire Houston market.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the median home price in Friendswood, TX?

The median home price in Friendswood is approximately $385,000 as of 2026. Prices range from the high $200s in established neighborhoods to over $650K in premier master-planned communities like Silverlake.

What school district is Friendswood TX in?

Friendswood is served by Friendswood ISD, one of the most highly regarded school districts in the Greater Houston area. The district is known for academic excellence, championship athletics, and strong community involvement.

What are the best neighborhoods in Friendswood TX?

Top neighborhoods include Silverlake (master-planned, golf, lakes), Heritage Park (established, mature trees), Forest Bend (family-friendly, great value), Rancho Viejo (larger lots, privacy), and Country Place (golf community).

Is Friendswood TX a good place to live?

Consistently ranked among the best cities in Texas, Friendswood offers top schools, low crime, a strong community culture, and excellent proximity to Houston, the Gulf Coast, and the Texas Medical Center.

How long does it take to sell a home in Friendswood?

Well-priced Friendswood homes typically sell in 21–28 days. Homes in Silverlake and Heritage Park with proper staging often receive multiple offers within the first week.
