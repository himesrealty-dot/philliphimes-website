The Rodeo Palms Experience

Manvel's most established community — amenities, Alvin ISD schools, and the area's best value

"Rodeo Palms is where Manvel buyers start and often end their search — a well-established community with real amenities, solid schools, and prices that still leave room in the budget for the rest of life."

Rodeo Palms is Manvel's most established master-planned community — built over time and settled in enough to have that neighborhood feeling that newer developments take years to develop. Community pools, a splash pad, walking trails, and scenic ponds give residents the everyday lifestyle infrastructure that matters for families.

The school story is Alvin ISD feeding into Manvel High School — the district's high school that serves this growing part of Brazoria County. For buyers choosing Manvel, the school assignment is a primary consideration, and Rodeo Palms delivers consistently on that front.

The value proposition — $235K to $500K, with a recent median around $321K — makes Rodeo Palms one of the most accessible master-planned communities in the greater South Houston area. Buyers who've looked at Pearland, League City, or Clear Lake often find that Manvel generally and Rodeo Palms specifically offer meaningfully more home for their money.

Common Questions

Rodeo Palms — frequently asked questions

What school district is Rodeo Palms in?
Rodeo Palms is zoned to Alvin Independent School District, feeding into Manvel High School. Always confirm the specific campus for your address before purchasing.
What amenities does Rodeo Palms have?
Rodeo Palms features a full amenity package: community pool, splash pad, walking trails, scenic ponds, and clubhouse — the most complete amenity offering of Manvel's established communities.
What is the price range in Rodeo Palms?
Home prices in Rodeo Palms range from approximately $235,000 to $500,000, with a recent median around $321,000 — one of the most accessible master-planned communities in the greater South Houston area. The most active range is typically $300K–$400K.
How far is Rodeo Palms from downtown Houston?
Rodeo Palms is approximately 35 minutes from downtown Houston via SH 288 or Highway 6 to I-45 — and about 30 minutes to the Texas Medical Center. Most residents work within Houston or in Pearland/Manvel's growing employment base.
Is Rodeo Palms the best community in Manvel?
Rodeo Palms is often considered Manvel's most established community — it has the most mature neighborhood character of Manvel's options, with well-developed amenities and settled streets. Newer communities like Del Bello Lakes and Sedona Lakes offer newer construction; Rodeo Palms offers more established character.
What are HOA fees in Rodeo Palms?
HOA fees in Rodeo Palms typically range from $650–$1,500 per year depending on the builder section, covering the community pool, splash pad, trails, and common area maintenance. Always verify the current amount before closing.
