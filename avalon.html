The Avalon Experience

Friendswood's only gated lakefront community — and it shows in every detail

"Avalon gives you something Friendswood hasn't had before: gated privacy, lakefront living, and luxury new construction all in one community — without leaving the school district that makes this city so desirable."

Launched in 2021, Avalon at Friendswood represents a new chapter for the city. Built by Taylor Morrison around a 36-acre lake, the community was designed from the ground up as a gated, resort-style neighborhood — the first of its kind in Friendswood. The lake pavilion anchors community life, offering kayaking, fishing, and waterfront walking paths that wind through the neighborhood.

Taylor Morrison's three collections — the 60s, 70s, and 90s series — give buyers a genuine range of options. Entry-level plans start in the mid-$600Ks with modern open-concept designs and quality finishes. The 70' and 90' lots push toward seven figures, often with lake views, extended outdoor living, and full upgrade packages. Every home in the community has full access to the resort-style amenities: pool, clubhouse, fitness center, pickleball and tennis courts.

What sets Avalon apart from other Friendswood communities isn't just the lake or the gated entry — it's the combination. Friendswood ISD schools, proximity to I-45 and FM 528, and a lifestyle that genuinely feels like a resort rather than just a subdivision. It's the neighborhood buyers move to Friendswood specifically for.

Common Questions

Avalon at Friendswood — frequently asked questions

What school district is Avalon Friendswood in?
Avalon at Friendswood is zoned to Friendswood ISD — consistently rated one of the top school districts in the Houston Bay Area. Known for strong academics, competitive athletics, and exceptional community involvement, Friendswood ISD is a major reason buyers choose this city. Always confirm the specific campus assignment for your address before purchasing.
What are the HOA fees in Avalon Friendswood?
Avalon HOA fees are approximately $158 per month (~$1,896 per year), plus a one-time capital contribution at closing. Fees cover gated security, resort-style pool, clubhouse, fitness center, pickleball and tennis courts, lake maintenance, trails, parks, and common area upkeep. Verify the current amount with the HOA or your agent before closing.
Who builds homes in Avalon at Friendswood?
Avalon at Friendswood is a Taylor Morrison community. Taylor Morrison offers three collections in the neighborhood: the 60s series (60-foot lots), the 70s series (70-foot lots), and the 90s series (90-foot lots, many lakefront). Floor plans range from approximately 2,500 to 5,000+ sq ft with luxury finishes and modern designs.
What is the price range for homes in Avalon?
Homes in Avalon at Friendswood range from the mid-$600Ks for 60' series entry plans to $1.2M+ for premium 90' lakefront homesites. The most active price range is $750K–$950K, representing the 70' and upgraded 60' collections with lake views and modern finishes.
Is Avalon at Friendswood a gated community?
Yes — Avalon at Friendswood is a fully gated community with controlled access at the entry. This provides an additional layer of privacy and security that is rare among Friendswood neighborhoods. The gated entry is one of the features that makes Avalon stand out in the local market.
Does Avalon have a lake?
Yes — Avalon is centered on a 36-acre lake with a lakeside pavilion. Residents enjoy kayaking, fishing, and waterfront trails. Many homes have lake-view or lake-adjacent settings, and the pavilion serves as a community gathering space. The lake is one of Avalon's most defining features and a primary reason buyers choose it over other Friendswood communities.
How far is Avalon Friendswood from downtown Houston?
Avalon at Friendswood is approximately 30 minutes from downtown Houston via I-45 North. NASA Johnson Space Center is about 20 minutes via FM 528, the Texas Medical Center is approximately 35 minutes, and Kemah Boardwalk is about 25 minutes.
What restaurants are near Avalon Friendswood?
Popular nearby dining includes B1895 Tavern and Locals Kitchen & Market (both at 607 S Friendswood Dr), Birraporetti's (400 W Parkwood Ave), Perry's Steakhouse (614 S Friendswood Dr), and P.F. Chang's (1454 W Bay Area Blvd). The Baybrook Mall corridor offers Maggiano's, Yard House, Rouxpour, and Whiskey Cake.
What grocery stores are near Avalon?
H-E-B (701 W Parkwood Ave) is the go-to for most Friendswood residents. Kroger at Autumn Creek (3135 E FM 528 Rd) is particularly convenient to Avalon's FM 528 location. Both stores offer full pharmacies, curbside pickup, and fuel centers.
What is it like to live in Avalon at Friendswood?
Avalon residents describe a quiet, resort-like lifestyle with the convenience of Friendswood's small-town charm and South Houston's major amenities. The gated entry, 36-acre lake, and modern Taylor Morrison homes attract buyers seeking luxury finishes and community privacy. Friendswood ISD schools and proximity to NASA and the Texas Medical Center make it particularly popular with professional families relocating to the area.
