The Riverstone Ranch Experience

Master-planned family living in Pearland — Alvin ISD schools and quick Houston access

"Riverstone Ranch delivers what Pearland families want most — a well-planned community with amenities, Shadow Creek High School, and a manageable Houston commute — at prices that actually work."

Riverstone Ranch is one of Pearland's well-regarded master-planned communities — built for families who want more than a subdivision. Community pools, walking trails, and maintained green spaces give residents the lifestyle infrastructure that makes a neighborhood feel complete rather than just a collection of houses.

The school story is strong: Alvin ISD's Shadow Creek High School serves most of Riverstone Ranch, and the district has built a solid reputation for academics and programs. For families whose school decision is central to their community choice, this is a consistent advantage.

Houston access is a practical draw. At approximately 25 minutes to downtown, Riverstone Ranch positions Pearland families within reasonable commuting distance of the Texas Medical Center, downtown Houston, and the broader employment corridor along SH 288.

Common Questions

Riverstone Ranch — frequently asked questions

What school district is Riverstone Ranch in?
Riverstone Ranch is zoned to Alvin Independent School District, feeding into Shadow Creek High School. Always confirm the specific campus assignment for your address before purchasing.
What amenities does Riverstone Ranch have?
Riverstone Ranch features master-planned community amenities including a community pool, recreation center, walking and biking trails, and maintained green spaces — giving residents the lifestyle infrastructure that typical subdivisions lack.
What is the price range in Riverstone Ranch?
Home prices range from approximately $315,000 to $600,000. The most active range is $330K–$430K, with a second tier of larger homes closing in the $500K–$560K range — all with full access to community amenities and Alvin ISD schools.
How far is Riverstone Ranch from downtown Houston?
Riverstone Ranch is approximately 25 minutes from downtown Houston via SH 288 — and about 20 minutes to the Texas Medical Center. This makes it one of the more Houston-accessible Pearland communities.
What are the HOA fees in Riverstone Ranch?
HOA fees in Riverstone Ranch typically range from $600–$1,200 per year, reflecting the community pool, trails, and maintained common areas. Always verify the current amount before closing.
Is there new construction available in Riverstone Ranch?
Riverstone Ranch has featured a mix of new construction and resale homes throughout its development. Depending on current phases, new construction may still be available — contact Phil to discuss current options and builder availability.
