The Bayou Bend Estates Experience

A brand-new Dickinson community with something most new construction doesn't have — its own boat ramp

"Bayou Bend Estates is what I point buyers to when they want a new home but don't want to give up the water. A private resident boat ramp on Dickinson Bayou, in a small community of new builds — that's a genuinely rare combination."

Bayou Bend Estates is a newer, small-scale new-construction community in Dickinson — roughly 80 planned homes built from 2021 onward by Adams Homes and Gehan Homes. This isn't a decades-old subdivision; it's a fresh build-out off Leslie's Way where most of the homes have gone up between 2021 and 2026.

The standout feature is real: the community has a private resident boat ramp on Dickinson Bayou, and some lots offer bayou views. For a buyer who wants a brand-new home and direct water access, that combination is hard to find at this price point. This is Dickinson Bayou — a quiet, winding coastal waterway — not a Galveston Bay canal system, and the ramp is for residents.

The homes themselves are spacious — generally 2,202 to 2,557 square feet with 4-bedroom floor plans, and six floor plans available through Adams Homes. Add Dickinson's lower Galveston County property taxes on top of a new build, and you've got a compelling package for families who want room to grow plus a true waterfront amenity.

Common Questions

Bayou Bend Estates — frequently asked questions

Is Bayou Bend Estates new construction?
Yes — Bayou Bend Estates is a new-construction community in Dickinson, built 2021 onward by Adams Homes and Gehan Homes. It is a smaller community of roughly 80 planned homes, with most homes built between 2021 and 2026.
Does Bayou Bend Estates have a boat ramp?
Yes — Bayou Bend Estates has a private resident boat ramp on Dickinson Bayou, and some lots have bayou views. The boat ramp gives residents direct water access from the community, which is rare at this price point.
What is the price range in Bayou Bend Estates?
Home prices in Bayou Bend Estates run roughly $330,000 to $400,000. Recent closed sales (a small sample of six homes from December 2025 to June 2026) landed in a tight band of about $333K to $364K with a median near $336K; Adams Homes markets the community from the mid $300s, with larger and upgraded plans pushing toward $400K.
What size are the homes in Bayou Bend Estates?
Homes in Bayou Bend Estates are spacious new builds, generally 2,202 to 2,557 square feet with 4-bedroom floor plans. Adams Homes offers six floor plans in the community.
What are the HOA fees in Bayou Bend Estates?
Bayou Bend Estates HOA fees are about $45 per month (roughly $540 per year). Always verify the current amount for any specific home before closing.
What school district is Bayou Bend Estates in?
Bayou Bend Estates is zoned to Dickinson ISD, feeding into Dickinson High School. Elementary and middle school assignments are address-specific — always confirm the specific campus assignment for any address before purchasing.
