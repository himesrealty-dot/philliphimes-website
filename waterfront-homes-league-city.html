Waterfront Living in the Bay Area

From Clear Lake to Galveston Bay

The Houston Bay Area sits at the intersection of Clear Lake, Taylor Lake, and Galveston Bay — one of the most active boating corridors on the Texas Gulf Coast. The waterfront real estate market here is genuinely diverse: marina-front townhomes in South Shore Harbour, quiet lakefront lots in Taylor Lake Village, lagoon-view new construction in Mar Bella, and new-construction bayou-front homes in Dickinson's Peacock Isle.

Each waterfront community has a completely different character. South Shore Harbour is the social hub — a full-service marina, yacht club, resort pool, and waterfront restaurants. Taylor Lake Village is serene and established — larger lots, mature trees, and the kind of neighborhood where neighbors wave from kayaks. Peacock Isle in Dickinson offers new-construction homes with direct Dickinson Bayou frontage at prices well below the League City waterfront market.

Phil covers all four communities and knows the flood zone picture, the HOA structure, and the dock access rules for each one. If you're buying waterfront, the details matter — and he'll walk you through every one before you're under contract.

What Phil Checks on Every Waterfront Purchase
  • FEMA flood zone designation and flood map panel number
  • Current NFIP flood insurance premium estimate
  • Elevation certificate (coordinates or orders one)
  • HOA dock rules and private vs. community slip access
  • Boat lift and pier permits — transferable or not
  • Bulkhead condition and maintenance responsibility
  • Water body depth at the dock (tidal variance, sedimentation)
  • Harvey / flood history disclosure review

Common Questions

Waterfront Home Buying FAQ

Waterfront purchases require a sharper level of due diligence than a standard home buy. Phil has done dozens of them. No question is too basic — ask it before you're under contract, not after.

Ask Phil →

Are there waterfront homes for sale in League City TX?

Yes — League City has two distinct waterfront communities. South Shore Harbour on Clear Lake is the flagship, with homes from $370K to $3M+ and a full marina. Mar Bella has lagoon and pond-front new construction lots from around $370K. North on Taylor Lake, the Taylor Lake Village area offers true lakefront in CCISD at $350K–$750K. Phil tracks active waterfront inventory across all communities.

What is the best waterfront neighborhood in League City?

South Shore Harbour is the most complete waterfront lifestyle in the Bay Area — full-service marina, yacht club, resort pool, and direct Galveston Bay access. If you want true lakefront with a quieter feel, Taylor Lake Village is the answer. If budget is the priority, Peacock Isle in Dickinson offers new-construction homes with direct Dickinson Bayou frontage starting around $340K. The right choice depends on whether you need boat slip access, how much community amenity matters, and your budget ceiling.

How much do waterfront homes cost in League City TX?

Waterfront pricing ranges from about $340K in Peacock Isle (Dickinson) to $1.3M+ in South Shore Harbour's custom estate section. League City waterfront (South Shore Harbour) starts around $305K for townhomes and rises steeply for direct-water homes with private docks. Taylor Lake Village lakefront runs $400K–$750K. Mar Bella lagoon-view new construction starts around $420K. Every waterfront sale carries a premium — typically 15–30% over comparable non-waterfront homes in the same community.

Does South Shore Harbour have marina and boat dock access?

Yes — South Shore Harbour has a full-service marina with boat slips, a yacht club, and a direct channel to Clear Lake and Galveston Bay. Many waterfront homes in SSH have private docks or access to community slips. The marina accommodates vessels up to several dozen feet, making it one of the few communities in the greater Houston area genuinely suited to serious boaters. Slip availability and transfer rules vary — Phil verifies these for each specific property before you make an offer.

What are flood zone considerations for waterfront homes near Galveston Bay?

Flood zone status varies significantly street by street — even within the same community. Many waterfront homes are in FEMA flood zones AE or X. Phil reviews the FEMA flood map panel, discloses NFIP insurance cost estimates, and recommends an independent elevation certificate before closing on any waterfront property. South Shore Harbour underwent significant flood mitigation work post-Harvey. Peacock Isle sits on Dickinson Bayou. Phil will not let a client close on waterfront without a clear eyes-open understanding of the flood picture.
