The Country Place Experience

Pearland's original 55+ active adult community — country club living with three decades of community

"Country Place is where Pearland's most active retirees choose to live. The country club, the golf, the pools — but more than that, it's the community. Three decades of neighbors who know each other by name. That's rare, and buyers who find it don't want to leave."

Country Place was established in the 1980s as one of the first dedicated active adult communities in the South Houston area. For over three decades, it has been the premier destination for buyers seeking the 55+ lifestyle in Pearland — a genuine country club community with on-site golf, dining, resort pools, tennis courts, and the kind of active social programming that keeps residents engaged year-round.

The community is age-restricted to buyers 55 and older, creating a neighborhood dynamic that is purposefully different from standard family communities. Residents consistently describe Country Place as a place where neighbors become friends, where the calendar is always full, and where the quality of daily life genuinely improves after moving in. The golf course and country club serve as the social hub, hosting events, dining, and activities that bring the community together.

Homes in Country Place are primarily resale properties built in the 1980s and 1990s — well-maintained single-story and two-story homes ranging from comfortable patio homes to larger properties for buyers who want space to host family. The central Pearland location near FM 518 provides excellent access to Broadway dining, HEB, medical facilities, and Hwy 288. For buyers entering retirement or the 55+ stage of life, Country Place offers one of the most complete lifestyle packages in the Houston Bay Area.

Common Questions

Country Place — frequently asked questions

What age restriction does Country Place Pearland have?
Country Place is an age-restricted 55+ active adult community. At least one resident per household must be 55 or older, and no permanent residents under a certain age (typically 18) are permitted. Verify the specific age restrictions with the HOA before purchasing.
Does Country Place have a golf course?
Yes — Country Place features an on-site country club with a golf course. The country club serves as the community's social hub and includes dining, events, and social programming. Country club membership fees are separate from HOA fees — verify the current structure with the HOA.
What are HOA fees in Country Place Pearland?
Country Place HOA fees are approximately $500–$900 per year for community maintenance, pools, and common areas. Country club membership involves additional fees depending on the membership level chosen. Verify all fee structures with the HOA and country club before closing.
What are home prices in Country Place?
Homes in Country Place range from approximately $250K for smaller patio homes to $500K+ for larger premium properties near the golf course. The most active range is $330K–$420K — mid-size single-story homes that represent the community's most popular configuration for active adult living.
What amenities does Country Place have?
Country Place features a country club, golf course, resort pool complex, tennis courts, and fitness facilities. The country club hosts a social calendar with events, dining, clubs, and activities year-round. It is one of the most comprehensively amenitized 55+ communities in the Greater Houston area.
How far is Country Place from medical facilities?
Country Place is approximately 20 minutes from the Texas Medical Center via Hwy 288 — excellent access for residents with healthcare needs. Kelsey-Seybold Clinic in Pearland is within a short drive, and UTMB Health in Webster is about 30 minutes.
What restaurants are near Country Place?
Country Place has the country club's own dining facilities plus easy access to all of Pearland's restaurant corridor — The Toasted Yolk, Fish City Grill, Lupe Tortilla, BJ's Brewhouse, and more are all within a short drive on Broadway.
What is the social scene like at Country Place?
Country Place has a genuinely active social community. Residents participate in golf leagues, swim programs, tennis clubs, social events at the country club, and neighborhood gatherings. Buyers who move to Country Place consistently cite the community and social life as exceeding their expectations.
Is Country Place a good value for 55+ buyers in Houston?
Country Place is consistently considered one of the best values for 55+ buyers in the Houston Bay Area — the combination of on-site country club, golf, resort pools, and central Pearland location at accessible price points is genuinely rare. Most comparable communities either lack the amenities or cost considerably more.
What does the buying process look like for a 55+ community?
Buying in a 55+ age-restricted community involves a few extra steps — the HOA reviews buyers for age qualification, and the purchase contract may include community-specific disclosures. I'm experienced with 55+ transactions and will make sure the process is smooth. Contact me directly to discuss the Country Place-specific requirements.
