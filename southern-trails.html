The Southern Trails Experience

Established Pearland community with genuine amenities — Shadow Creek High and SH 288 access

"Southern Trails has what a lot of Pearland communities claim to have but don't quite deliver — a real amenity center, walkable trails, scenic ponds, and Shadow Creek High School within a well-established neighborhood."

Southern Trails is one of Pearland's established communities that has genuinely earned its reputation. The amenity package — community pool, splash pad, clubhouse, walking trails, and scenic ponds throughout the neighborhood — creates the kind of everyday lifestyle that families actually use, not just amenities on a flyer.

Alvin ISD's Shadow Creek High School anchors the educational story. For Pearland families who've done the research on local school options, Shadow Creek is consistently among the most sought-after high school assignments in the area.

The price range — $310K to $520K — positions Southern Trails in Pearland's mid-market, offering families meaningfully more home and neighborhood character than entry-level options while remaining accessible compared to premium Clear Lake or League City communities.

Common Questions

Southern Trails — frequently asked questions

What school district is Southern Trails in?
Southern Trails is zoned to Alvin Independent School District, feeding into Shadow Creek High School. Always confirm the specific campus assignment for your address before purchasing.
What amenities does Southern Trails have?
Southern Trails features a genuine amenity package: community pool, splash pad, clubhouse, walking trails, and scenic ponds throughout the neighborhood. These are amenities residents actually use, not just marketing bullet points.
What is the price range in Southern Trails?
Home prices in Southern Trails range from approximately $310,000 to $520,000. The most active range is typically $390K–$460K for updated homes, often with pond-backing lots available.
How far is Southern Trails from downtown Houston?
Southern Trails is approximately 25 minutes from downtown Houston via SH 288, and about 20 minutes to the Texas Medical Center — making it practical for both downtown and medical district professionals.
Are there pond-front lots available in Southern Trails?
Yes — Southern Trails has numerous pond-front and pond-backing lots scattered throughout the neighborhood. These tend to command a modest premium but offer excellent privacy and views. Contact Phil for available inventory.
What are HOA fees in Southern Trails?
HOA fees in Southern Trails typically range from $700–$1,200 per year, covering the community pool, splash pad, clubhouse, trail maintenance, and common areas. Always verify the current amount before closing.
