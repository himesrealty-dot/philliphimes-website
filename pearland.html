Local Knowledge

Living in Pearland, TX

Pearland has grown from a small agricultural community into one of the most dynamic cities in Texas — and it's done so without losing its livability. With over 130,000 residents and counting, it now offers the amenities of a major suburb while maintaining neighborhood-level character in most of its communities.

Its location is its superpower. Pearland sits directly south of the Texas Medical Center — one of the world's largest medical complexes — making it the top choice for physicians, nurses, researchers, and support staff who want an easy commute without paying Houston prices. The Beltway 8 and Highway 288 corridors put downtown Houston within 20–25 minutes.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the median home price in Pearland, TX?

The median home price in Pearland is approximately $320,000 as of 2026. New construction in communities like Meridiana and Pomona starts in the low $300s, while established neighborhoods like Southgate offer entry points in the high $200s.

What school district is Pearland TX in?

Most of Pearland is served by Pearland ISD, a highly regarded district known for strong academics and athletics. Some southern portions of Pearland fall within Alvin ISD.

What are the best neighborhoods in Pearland TX?

Top neighborhoods include Shadow Creek Ranch (flagship master-planned), Silverlake (golf and lakes), Southgate (established value), Meridiana (new construction), and Pomona (modern master-planned).

Why do people move to Pearland?

Pearland's combination of affordability, top schools, proximity to the Texas Medical Center, and master-planned community lifestyle makes it one of the most popular relocation destinations in the entire Houston metro.

How long does it take to sell a home in Pearland?

Well-priced Pearland homes typically sell in 25–35 days. Shadow Creek Ranch and newer communities like Meridiana often see multiple offers on move-in ready homes within the first two weeks.
