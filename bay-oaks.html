The Bay Oaks Experience

Clear Lake's premier established neighborhood — where aerospace professionals put down roots

"Bay Oaks is where the oak trees are taller than the houses, the lots are wide enough to breathe, and your neighbors probably work at NASA or Boeing. It has a permanence that newer communities can't fake."

Bay Oaks is one of Clear Lake's most established and sought-after neighborhoods — built in the 1970s and 1980s at the height of the Space Age and home ever since to aerospace engineers, astronauts, and NASA professionals who chose it because it's simply one of the best places to live in the Houston Bay Area.

The neighborhood's defining characteristic is its oak tree canopy. Decades of growth have produced streets shaded by massive live oaks, giving Bay Oaks a natural beauty that separates it immediately from newer planned developments. Lots are generous — typically a quarter-acre or more — giving homes room to breathe and owners space to customize.

Clear Creek ISD's Clear Lake High School draws families who prioritize academics and extracurricular excellence. The school's strong reputation, combined with Bay Oaks' proximity to NASA and the Bay Area's professional employment base, makes this neighborhood one of the most consistently in-demand addresses in Clear Lake.

Common Questions

Bay Oaks — frequently asked questions

What school district is Bay Oaks in?
Bay Oaks is zoned to Clear Creek Independent School District (CCISD), feeding into Clear Lake High School. CCISD is consistently ranked among the top school districts in the Houston area and Texas. Always confirm the specific campus assignment for your address before purchasing.
How close is Bay Oaks to NASA?
Bay Oaks is approximately 5 minutes from NASA Johnson Space Center — one of the primary reasons the neighborhood has historically attracted aerospace engineers, scientists, and NASA employees. Many residents can and do walk or bike to JSC.
What is the price range in Bay Oaks?
Home prices in Bay Oaks range from approximately $380,000 for classic original homes to over $700,000 for fully renovated properties. The most active range is $480K–$590K for updated move-in ready homes.
What are the HOA fees in Bay Oaks?
Bay Oaks HOA fees typically range from $600–$1,200 per year depending on the section. Always verify the current amount for the specific section before closing.
What makes Bay Oaks different from other Clear Lake neighborhoods?
Bay Oaks is distinguished by its mature oak tree canopy, larger lot sizes, deeply established community character, and its NASA-adjacent location. Newer Clear Lake neighborhoods can't replicate the decades of tree growth and neighborhood culture that Bay Oaks has developed.
How far is Bay Oaks from downtown Houston?
Bay Oaks is approximately 30 minutes from downtown Houston via I-45 — a reasonable commute for professionals who also value being 5 minutes from NASA and 15 minutes from Kemah's waterfront.
