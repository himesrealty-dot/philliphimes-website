The Reserve at Clear Lake Experience

Clear Lake's premier gated community — luxury homes with top CCISD schools and NASA access

"The Reserve delivers what Clear Lake's most discerning buyers want: gated security, luxury finishes, top-rated schools, and a NASA commute measured in minutes — not compromises."

The Reserve at Clear Lake sits at the top of the Clear Lake residential market — a gated community with luxury homes, community amenities, and the full complement of CCISD schools including Clear Lake High School. For buyers who want privacy and security alongside the best schools in the area, The Reserve is a consistent first conversation.

The homes here are built to a higher standard than most of the Clear Lake market — larger square footage, premium finishes, and layouts that reflect the expectations of buyers in the $420K–$900K range. The community's gated access and maintained common areas create an environment that's distinctly different from typical suburban subdivisions.

NASA proximity remains a core part of the value equation: 10–15 minutes to Johnson Space Center makes The Reserve practical for senior aerospace and engineering professionals who want their home to reflect their success without adding an hour to their workday.

Common Questions

The Reserve at Clear Lake — frequently asked questions

Is The Reserve at Clear Lake a gated community?
Yes — The Reserve at Clear Lake features gated access and controlled entry, providing residents with enhanced security, privacy, and the distinct character of a true luxury community within the Clear Lake area.
What school district is The Reserve at Clear Lake in?
The Reserve is zoned to Clear Creek Independent School District (CCISD), feeding into Clear Lake High School. CCISD is one of the top school districts in the Houston metro. Always confirm the specific campus for your address before purchasing.
What is the price range in The Reserve at Clear Lake?
Home prices range from approximately $420,000 to $900,000+, reflecting the community's gated character, luxury finishes, larger floor plans, and premium location within the Clear Lake market.
What are HOA fees like in The Reserve?
HOA fees in The Reserve are higher than typical Clear Lake neighborhoods — reflecting the gated access, maintained common areas, community pool, and amenities. Always verify the current amount before closing.
How far is The Reserve from NASA?
The Reserve is approximately 10–15 minutes from NASA Johnson Space Center — well within the NASA corridor that draws aerospace and engineering professionals throughout the Bay Area.
How far is The Reserve from downtown Houston?
The Reserve at Clear Lake is approximately 30 minutes from downtown Houston via I-45 or Beltway 8 — consistent with the broader Clear Lake area. Most residents prioritize the gated lifestyle, CCISD schools, and NASA access.
