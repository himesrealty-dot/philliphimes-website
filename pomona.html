The Pomona Experience

SW Pearland resort living — resort-style pools, nature trails, and luxury community design

"Pomona redefined what a Pearland master-planned community could look like. The cabana-lined pools stop people in their tracks, but it's the preserved natural areas and the trails along Mustang Bayou that make people want to stay forever."

Pomona launched in 2015 as one of Pearland's most visually striking master-planned communities, developed by Hillwood Communities in SW Pearland near Hwy 288 and FM 518. The community made an immediate statement with an amenity center built around resort-style pools — cabana-lined, with a splash pad for the kids and a fishing area nearby. That design philosophy extends throughout: nature trails winding along Mustang Bayou and through preserved natural areas, community ponds, a fitness center, and a level of landscaping investment that signals a different caliber of suburban community.

Homes in Pomona span a wide range and are heavily new construction. Entry-level homes start in the $365Ks; larger floor plans and premium lots push past $1.2M. The community continues to see active new construction, giving buyers the option of both resale and new builds depending on their timeline and preferences.

Pomona is zoned to Alvin ISD, feeding into Pomona Elementary (located within the community), Rodeo Palms Junior High, and Manvel High School. Buyers who specifically need Pearland ISD should confirm zoning before purchasing. For buyers open to Alvin ISD schools and drawn by the community's resort amenities and newer construction, Pomona offers one of the best lifestyle packages in the Pearland area.

Common Questions

Pomona — frequently asked questions

What school district is Pomona Pearland in?
Pomona is zoned to Alvin ISD, not Pearland ISD. The community feeds into Pomona Elementary (located within the community), Rodeo Palms Junior High, and Manvel High School. Buyers who specifically require Pearland ISD should verify zoning before purchasing. Campus assignments are address-specific.
Does Pomona have an elementary school in the community?
Yes — Pomona Elementary School is located within the community, making it walkable for elementary-aged children. This on-site school is one of Pomona's standout features and a major draw for families with young children.
What amenities does Pomona have?
Pomona, built by Hillwood Communities, centers on an amenity center with resort-style pools lined with cabanas plus a splash pad for the kids. Beyond the pools you'll find a fitness center, a fishing area, and miles of nature trails that follow Mustang Bayou and wind through the community's preserved natural areas.
What are home prices in Pomona Pearland?
Homes in Pomona range from the $365Ks for entry-level newer construction up to $1.2M+ for premium floor plans on the best lots. The most active band runs roughly $420K–$700K, with a median around $490K. Because Pomona is heavily new construction by Hillwood Communities, prices reflect modern finishes and layouts.
Are there nature trails in Pomona?
Yes — Pomona features an extensive nature trail system that winds through preserved wetland areas and natural landscapes. The trails are one of the community's defining features, offering a distinctly different outdoor experience from the typical suburban walking path.
How far is Pomona from the Texas Medical Center?
Pomona is approximately 25 minutes from the Texas Medical Center via Hwy 288 North — a direct, straightforward commute. This makes Pomona one of the most convenient communities in the Pearland area for TMC professionals.
What restaurants are near Pomona Pearland?
Pomona residents enjoy Pearland's growing restaurant scene — Lupe Tortilla, BJ's Brewhouse, Fish City Grill, Gringo's Mexican Kitchen, and Killen's Texas BBQ are all within easy reach on the Broadway corridor.
What makes Pomona different from Shadow Creek Ranch?
Pomona (2015+) is newer than Shadow Creek Ranch (2000s) and features a more modern design aesthetic with its resort-style pools and preserved natural areas. Pomona tends to have more contemporary floor plans and finishes. Both are Alvin ISD communities, but Pomona's Hwy 288 location gives it better direct TMC access.
What grocery stores are near Pomona?
H-E-B (6120 W Broadway Blvd) and Kroger (2405 Business Center Dr) are the primary grocery options. Both are within a short drive and offer full pharmacies, curbside pickup, and fuel centers.
Is Pomona a good investment in the Pearland market?
Pomona has strong investment fundamentals — newer construction (2015+), resort-caliber amenities, Hwy 288 access, and continued new development activity. The on-site elementary school and nature trail system add long-term lifestyle value. Resale values have held well, and the community continues to attract buyers at multiple price points.
