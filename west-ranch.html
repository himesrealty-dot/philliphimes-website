The West Ranch Experience

West Friendswood's most complete master-planned community — trails, pools, parks, and FISD schools

"West Ranch delivers what families actually want — connected walking trails, multiple resort pools, and Friendswood ISD schools — in a west Friendswood location that keeps commutes manageable and the neighborhood feel real."

West Ranch began in the late 2000s and grew through the 2010s into Friendswood's most comprehensive master-planned community. Developed by Friendswood Development Company across a 766-acre tract on the west side of the city, West Ranch was designed from the start with families in mind — multiple amenity centers, resort-style pools, connected trail systems, and open parks distributed throughout the neighborhood rather than concentrated in one corner. A planned 20-acre Town Center will add retail and dining within the community.

The community hosts a diverse range of home styles, from traditional brick homes in the $500Ks to larger new and near-new builds pushing toward $1.4M+. Top builders including Highland Homes, Perry Homes, and Village Builders have contributed over the years, creating variety in floor plans and architectural styles while maintaining a cohesive neighborhood aesthetic. The mix of resale and newer builds gives buyers flexibility in both price and condition.

West Ranch's location on the western edge of Friendswood puts residents near FM 518 and SH 35, providing good access to League City, Alvin, and I-45. Friendswood ISD schools, the primary reason many buyers target this ZIP code, are fully accessible from West Ranch. The community's trail system connects to parks and green spaces, creating a genuinely walkable neighborhood feel that residents consistently cite as a top reason they chose West Ranch.

Common Questions

West Ranch — frequently asked questions

What school district is West Ranch Friendswood in?
West Ranch is zoned to Friendswood ISD — one of the most respected school districts in the Houston Bay Area. The community typically feeds into Cline Elementary, Friendswood Intermediate, and Friendswood High School. Campus assignments are address-specific — always verify before purchasing.
What are HOA fees in West Ranch?
HOA fees in West Ranch are approximately $600–$1,200 per year, covering the community's resort-style pools, recreation centers, trail maintenance, parks, and common area upkeep. There may also be sub-association fees depending on the specific section. Verify the current amount with the HOA before closing.
What is the price range for homes in West Ranch Friendswood?
West Ranch homes range from the $500Ks for resale entry-level properties to $1.4M+ for premium homes in sections like Lakeside, The Reserve, and Sierra. The most active price segment is $600K–$800K (median sale price around $690K) — the 4–5 bedroom range with updated finishes and trail or pond access.
Does West Ranch have a pool?
Yes — West Ranch features multiple resort-style pools and amenity centers distributed throughout the community. Splash pads, recreation areas, and covered pavilions are also part of the amenity package. The connected trail system runs between amenity centers, making the community exceptionally walkable.
How big is West Ranch Friendswood?
West Ranch is one of Friendswood's largest master-planned communities, with hundreds of homes spread across multiple sections developed through the 2010s. The community is large enough to have multiple amenity centers and a connected trail network while maintaining a neighborhood feel through intentional park and green space placement.
How far is West Ranch from downtown Houston?
West Ranch is approximately 30 minutes from downtown Houston via I-45 North. NASA Johnson Space Center is about 25 minutes, the Texas Medical Center approximately 35 minutes, and Kemah Boardwalk about 30 minutes.
What builders built homes in West Ranch?
West Ranch was developed by Friendswood Development Company, with homes built by top Houston builders including Highland Homes, Perry Homes, and Village Builders across the neighborhood's phases. This gives West Ranch more design variety than single-builder communities.
Are there trails in West Ranch?
Yes — West Ranch has an extensive connected trail system that is one of its most popular features. Trails wind through parks, around ponds, and between amenity centers, creating a walkable community network. The trails also connect to Friendswood's broader trail system, including Heritage Park Trail.
What restaurants are near West Ranch?
West Ranch is a short drive from Friendswood's best dining — B1895 Tavern and Locals Kitchen & Market, BJ's Brewhouse (1640 W Bay Area Blvd), Perry's & Sons, and the Bay Area Blvd / Baybrook corridor with Maggiano's, Yard House, Whiskey Cake, and more.
Is West Ranch a good place to buy in Friendswood?
West Ranch is consistently one of Friendswood's most sought-after master-planned communities — strong resale values, excellent Friendswood ISD schools, resort amenities, and a well-maintained neighborhood aesthetic. It tends to hold value well and attracts buyers who want the full master-planned experience at a price point below newer luxury communities.
