The Brittany Lakes Experience

An established community that earns its reputation every single day

"Brittany Lakes has what newer communities are trying to build: real shade, real character, neighbors who've been here for years, and water you can actually get on. You don't find that combination at this price anywhere else in League City."

Newer master-planned communities can build a pool and call themselves a lakeside community. Brittany Lakes has three actual lakes — kayakable, fishable, and beautiful from your backyard. The mature trees lining every street took decades to grow, and no amount of new construction can replicate what time does to a neighborhood.

The resort pool and splash pad keep families close to home all summer. The walking trails wind through the community past the lakes and greenbelts, getting real daily use from residents who chose this neighborhood partly because of them. And the HOA — one of the lowest in any established League City community — means more of your monthly budget stays in your pocket.

CCISD schools, straightforward freeway access, and a price range that starts under $300K make Brittany Lakes one of the smartest buys in League City for families who want value without sacrifice. This is where people who did their homework end up.

Common Questions

Brittany Lakes — frequently asked questions

What school district is Brittany Lakes in?
Brittany Lakes is fully within Clear Creek ISD (CCISD) — one of the top school districts in the Houston Bay Area. The community feeds into Clear Springs High School. Always confirm the specific school assignment for any address before purchasing.
What are the HOA fees in Brittany Lakes?
Brittany Lakes HOA fees are approximately $600–$750 per year — one of the lowest annual HOA fees in any established League City neighborhood. This makes the community unusually attractive for buyers who want resort amenities without paying a premium to maintain them. Always verify the exact current amount before closing.
Can you kayak in Brittany Lakes?
Yes — Brittany Lakes has three lakes available for kayaking and fishing. This is one of the neighborhood's most distinctive and actively used features. Weekend mornings you'll regularly see residents out on the water, and the lakes are accessible for fishing year-round. It's genuinely rare to find this in a community at this price point.
What is the price range for homes in Brittany Lakes?
Home prices in Brittany Lakes range from approximately $300,000 at the entry level to over $700,000 for premium lakefront properties. The most active band is $420K–$550K, where buyers find well-maintained homes on mature lots — often with greenbelt or water views — at some of the best price-per-square-foot in established League City.
What amenities does Brittany Lakes have?
Brittany Lakes features three kayakable and fishable lakes, a resort-style pool with splash pad, miles of walking and biking trails, mature tree-lined streets, greenbelts, and open spaces. The amenity-to-HOA-fee ratio is one of the best in League City — residents access full resort amenities for roughly $650 per year.
How far is Brittany Lakes from downtown Houston?
Brittany Lakes is approximately 30 minutes from downtown Houston via I-45 North. The neighborhood's League City location provides good freeway access for commuters heading to Houston, NASA, UTMB, or the Texas City/Baytown petrochemical corridor.
What is it like to live in Brittany Lakes?
Brittany Lakes has the feel of a genuinely established neighborhood — with residents who've been there long enough to know each other, mature trees that shade the streets in summer, and amenities (the lakes especially) that younger communities simply can't replicate. It draws buyers who want real value: top schools, lake access, trails, a pool, and a low HOA — all without paying the Mar Bella premium. For a lot of people who research carefully, Brittany Lakes is the destination they didn't know they were looking for.
What is the closest hospital to Brittany Lakes?
The closest major hospital is UTMB Health League City Campus at 2240 Gulf Fwy S — approximately 12 minutes from Brittany Lakes. It is a full University of Texas Medical Branch facility with a Level II trauma center and 24-hour emergency services. Kelsey-Seybold Clinic (3625 E League City Pkwy) provides multi-specialty outpatient care nearby.
What grocery stores are near Brittany Lakes?
H-E-B (2955 Gulf Fwy, League City) and Kroger (1920 W League City Pkwy) are both within a short drive. CVS and Walgreens cover pharmacy and convenience needs along the League City Pkwy corridor. Most grocery runs take under 10 minutes.
