Why Aerospace Workers Choose Clear Lake & League City

NASA Johnson Space Center has anchored the Clear Lake real estate market since Mission Control moved here in 1961. The result is a market unlike anywhere else in Texas: a highly educated workforce, strong demand for quality schools, and neighborhoods that have been specifically built around the aerospace community's needs and lifestyle.

For incoming NASA civil servants, contractors, and aerospace industry workers relocating to the Houston area, the Clear Lake and League City markets are the default choice — and for good reason. You get A-rated schools in CCISD, a short commute, proximity to Clear Lake and Galveston Bay, and a tight-knit community of people who work in the same industry.

Phil has helped dozens of aerospace workers and NASA contractors find the right home in this market. He understands the tradeoffs between Clear Lake's 10-minute commute and League City's newer construction, wider price range, and more suburban feel — and he can help you make the right call for your situation.

NASA Area Home Buying FAQ

Relocating to the area for NASA or an aerospace contractor? Phil has helped dozens of incoming workers navigate this market. Every question gets a straight answer.

Ask Phil →

What neighborhoods are closest to NASA JSC?

The neighborhoods closest to JSC are in Clear Lake — Bay Oaks, Bay Forest, Bay Knoll, Brookwood, Taylor Lake Village, and The Reserve at Clear Lake — all within 10–15 minutes via NASA Road 1. League City's northern neighborhoods (South Shore Harbour, Mar Bella, Magnolia Creek) add 5–10 more minutes but offer newer construction and a wider price range.

How far is League City from NASA Johnson Space Center?

League City is 15–25 minutes from JSC depending on which part of the city. Northern League City neighborhoods near I-45 and NASA Road 1 (South Shore Harbour, Mar Bella, Magnolia Creek) run about 20 minutes. Southern League City neighborhoods like Tuscan Lakes are closer to 25–30 minutes. The tradeoff: League City offers more new construction, wider price ranges, and more suburban space.

What school district serves neighborhoods near NASA?

Clear Creek ISD serves virtually the entire NASA-area market — Clear Lake, League City, Nassau Bay, Webster, and most of Friendswood. CCISD is A-rated by the Texas Education Agency and consistently ranks among the top 10 districts in Greater Houston. Five high schools serve different zones; Phil verifies the exact campus assignment for every home.

Should I buy in Clear Lake or League City if I work at JSC?

It depends on your priorities. Clear Lake gives you 10 minutes to JSC and an established neighborhood feel — mature trees, older homes with character, and proximity to Clear Lake itself. League City gives you 20–25 minutes but newer construction, master-planned amenities, and a wider price range from $300K to $2M+. Phil can walk you through both markets side by side based on your budget and lifestyle.

Can I buy a home near NASA remotely before relocating?

Yes — Phil regularly works with buyers relocating from other cities and states. He can do virtual tours, provide detailed neighborhood video walkthroughs, coordinate remote showings, and guide you through the full process digitally. He's helped buyers close from California, Florida, Colorado, and Washington without setting foot in Texas until moving day.
