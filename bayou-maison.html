The Bayou Maison Experience

Bayou-side tranquility with a charming character — and Galveston County value

"There's a slower rhythm here. The bayou runs nearby, the streets are quiet, and the neighborhood has a personality that took decades to develop. That's not something you can build overnight."

Bayou Maison sits along Dickinson Bayou with a community character that's distinctly its own — relaxed, established, and rooted in the kind of neighborhood feel that's become increasingly rare in the Houston growth corridor. Homes here reflect decades of thoughtful living, with mature landscaping and a pace that sets it apart from newer developments.

Select bayou-front properties give residents direct water adjacency — morning walks along the bayou, views that change with the seasons, and a connection to the natural landscape that suburban sprawl rarely provides. The Dickinson Bayou is a living part of the community here, not a drainage feature.

Galveston County's tax advantage makes Bayou Maison's already-competitive pricing even more compelling. At $255K–$400K, buyers get established homes with genuine character at prices well below comparable waterway-adjacent properties in Harris County — while remaining within easy reach of Houston employment and Galveston's coastal amenities.

Common Questions

Bayou Maison — frequently asked questions

What school district is Bayou Maison in?
Bayou Maison is zoned to Dickinson Independent School District (DISD). The typical feeder pattern runs through Dunbar Middle School and Dickinson High School. The specific elementary campus can vary by address — always confirm before purchasing.
Is Bayou Maison close to the water?
Yes — Bayou Maison sits along Dickinson Bayou. Select bayou-front properties have direct water adjacency and scenic bayou views. Most interior homes are within walking distance of the bayou, giving the neighborhood a water-adjacent character throughout.
What is the price range in Bayou Maison?
Home prices in Bayou Maison range from approximately $255,000 for interior homes to around $400,000 for bayou-front properties with water views. The most active price band is $300K–$350K.
How far is Bayou Maison from Galveston and Kemah?
Bayou Maison is approximately 22 minutes from Galveston Island via I-45 South and 18 minutes from Kemah Boardwalk — making both beach trips and waterfront dining convenient regular outings.
What are the HOA fees in Bayou Maison?
Bayou Maison HOA fees are approximately $200–$400 per year. Always verify the current amount with the HOA or community association before closing.
How do Galveston County taxes compare to Harris County?
Galveston County property tax rates are generally lower than comparable Harris County addresses — often $1,000–$2,000 per year less on similarly priced homes. Always verify current rates with the Galveston Central Appraisal District.
