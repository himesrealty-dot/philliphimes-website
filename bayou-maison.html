The Bayou Maison Experience

A gated new-construction community in Dickinson — modern homes, walkable schools, Galveston County value

"Bayou Maison is the rare thing in Dickinson — a gated community of brand-new homes you can still get under $340K. You walk the kids to school, you're behind a gate, and your tax bill is Galveston County, not Harris. That combination is hard to find."

Bayou Maison is a gated new-construction community — not an older established neighborhood. The homes here were built between 2019 and 2025 by Adams Homes (the primary builder), Colina Homes, and Censeo Homes, so nearly everything is brand new or close to it. These are modern single-family floor plans, generally one-story and up, ranging from about 1,503 to 2,335 square feet.

The community takes its name from nearby Dickinson Bayou, and a handful of lots back to a bayou view. But let me be straight with you: this is an inland gated community, not a waterfront one. There are no canals and no boat docks. The real draw is what's inside the gate — new construction, quiet streets, and walking paths that connect to Dickinson ISD campuses, so a lot of families here can walk their kids to school.

Galveston County's tax advantage makes the pricing even more compelling. At $240K–$340K, buyers get a brand-new home behind a gate at prices well below comparable new construction in Harris County — while staying within easy reach of Houston employment, Kemah, and Galveston's coast.

Common Questions

Bayou Maison — frequently asked questions

What school district is Bayou Maison in?
Bayou Maison is zoned to Dickinson Independent School District (DISD). The feeder pattern runs through Hughes Road Elementary, Dunbar Middle School, and Dickinson High School — and homes are walkable to Dickinson ISD campuses. School campus assignments are address-specific — always confirm the specific assignment for any address before purchasing.
Is Bayou Maison a new-construction community?
Yes — Bayou Maison is a gated new-construction community built from 2019 through 2025 by Adams Homes (the primary builder), Colina Homes, and Censeo Homes. Homes are modern single-family floor plans ranging from roughly 1,503 to 2,335 square feet.
What are the HOA fees in Bayou Maison?
Bayou Maison HOA dues run roughly $350 per year in the Adams Homes section up to about $700 per year in the Colina Homes section, depending on the builder section. Always verify the current amount for a specific home before closing.
Is Bayou Maison near the water?
Bayou Maison sits near Dickinson Bayou — that's where the name comes from — and a small number of lots have a bayou view. It is an inland gated community, not a waterfront one: there are no canals or boat docks. The main draw is gated new construction with walking paths to Dickinson ISD schools.
What is the price range in Bayou Maison?
Home prices in Bayou Maison range from approximately $240,000 to $340,000. Recent closed sales (December 2025–June 2026) ranged from $243,000 to $319,000 with a median of $291,000, and most active listings sit in the $275K–$310K band.
What are Galveston County taxes like?
Galveston County property tax rates are generally lower than comparable Harris County addresses — typically $1,000–$2,000 per year less on similarly priced homes. Always verify current rates with the Galveston Central Appraisal District.
