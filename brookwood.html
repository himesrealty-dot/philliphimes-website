The Brookwood Experience

Established Clear Lake living with mature character — CCISD schools and NASA-corridor access

"Brookwood buyers get what they came to Clear Lake for — top-rated schools, NASA proximity, and a neighborhood that actually feels lived-in — at prices that make sense for real families."

Brookwood is one of Clear Lake's quietly dependable established neighborhoods — a community with mature canopy trees, well-maintained streets, and the sort of neighborhood character that only decades can build. For buyers who want the full Clear Lake experience without reaching for the top of the market, Brookwood consistently delivers.

The school story is strong: Clear Creek ISD is one of the top-rated districts in the Houston metro, and Brookwood feeds into Clear Lake High School. For families prioritizing educational quality alongside the NASA-corridor lifestyle, Brookwood checks all the boxes.

The price range — $340K to $565K — puts Brookwood solidly in Clear Lake's mid-market. Buyers get more for their money here than in neighboring premium communities while sharing the same school district and the same short commute to NASA JSC.

Common Questions

Brookwood — frequently asked questions

What school district is Brookwood in?
Brookwood is zoned to Clear Creek Independent School District (CCISD), feeding into Clear Lake High School. CCISD is one of the top school districts in the Houston area. Always confirm the specific campus assignment for your address before purchasing.
What is the price range in Brookwood?
Home prices in Brookwood range from approximately $340,000 to $565,000, offering solid mid-range value in the Clear Lake market with CCISD schools. The most active range is typically $400K–$540K for updated move-in ready homes.
How close is Brookwood to NASA?
Brookwood is approximately 10–12 minutes from NASA Johnson Space Center, making it a practical choice for aerospace professionals who want established neighborhood character with a short commute.
What are the HOA fees in Brookwood?
Brookwood HOA fees typically range from $400–$700 per year. Always verify the current amount before closing.
Is Brookwood a good neighborhood to buy in?
Yes — Brookwood offers solid established value in the Clear Lake market. Buyers get top-rated CCISD schools including Clear Lake High School, NASA proximity, and mature neighborhood character at mid-range prices. It's consistently a strong choice for families and aerospace professionals.
How far is Brookwood from downtown Houston?
Brookwood is approximately 30 minutes from downtown Houston via I-45 — standard for the Clear Lake area. Most Brookwood residents prioritize the NASA corridor and CCISD schools over minimizing the Houston commute.
