The Valencia Experience

Manvel's growing master-planned community — newer construction, modern living, and Alvin ISD schools

"Valencia buyers are getting what buyers in Pearland or League City wish they could find — newer construction with modern floor plans, community amenities, and Alvin ISD schools, all at prices that are meaningfully lower than comparable communities further north."

Valencia is one of Manvel's growing master-planned communities — a newer neighborhood with the modern floor plans and community infrastructure that buyers who've shopped established resale inventory often find difficult to locate at reasonable prices. Open-concept layouts, higher ceilings, and move-in ready finishes without the renovation premium.

The community amenities — pool, walking trails, and green spaces throughout the neighborhood — give Valencia the everyday lifestyle infrastructure that families want. It's the kind of community where neighbors meet at the pool, kids ride bikes on the trails, and the neighborhood feels active and engaged from the moment you move in.

The Alvin ISD assignment into Manvel High School is the same school pipeline as Rodeo Palms, Del Bello Lakes, and Sedona Lakes — consistent for buyers who've already decided Manvel is their city and are now choosing between communities. Valencia adds the newer construction angle to that comparison, which for many buyers is the deciding factor.

Common Questions

Valencia — frequently asked questions

What school district is Valencia in?
Valencia is zoned to Alvin Independent School District, feeding into Manvel High School. Always confirm the specific campus for your address before purchasing.
Is the construction in Valencia newer?
Yes — Valencia is a newer master-planned community with more recently built homes featuring modern open-concept floor plans, higher ceilings, and updated finishes. It's a strong option for buyers who want to avoid the renovation math that often comes with older resale inventory in the South Houston area.
What is the price range in Valencia?
Home prices in Valencia range from approximately $365,000 to $1.2M+, with a recent median around $510,000. The most active range is $450K–$700K for newer homes with modern finishes.
How does Valencia compare to other Manvel communities?
Valencia, Rodeo Palms, Del Bello Lakes, and Sedona Lakes are all Manvel communities with Alvin ISD schools. The main distinction for Valencia is its newer construction focus — buyers who specifically want modern floor plans and newer builds often land here. Rodeo Palms offers the most established character. Del Bello Lakes emphasizes lakefront living. Sedona Lakes leads on resort-style amenities. Contact Phil for a detailed comparison.
How far is Valencia from downtown Houston?
Valencia is approximately 35 minutes from downtown Houston via SH 288 or Highway 6 to I-45 — and about 30 minutes to the Texas Medical Center.
What are HOA fees in Valencia?
HOA fees in Valencia typically range from $600–$1,000 per year, covering community amenities and common area maintenance. Always verify the current amount before closing.
