Common Questions

NewBuildIQ FAQ

What is NewBuildIQ?

It's how Phil tracks new construction with real data: which builder incentives are live right now, what new homes are actually selling for, and which communities have current inventory. Most agents speak in generalities; NewBuildIQ runs on current, verified MLS data so your decisions are based on facts.

How much does it cost to work with Phil on new construction?

Nothing. The builder pays the buyer's agent fee, so Phil's representation costs you zero extra. Your price is the same whether you bring a REALTOR® or walk in alone — so there's no reason not to have an expert in your corner.

Why register with an agent before visiting a model home?

The on-site sales agent represents the builder, not you. If you register or tour without your own REALTOR®, you may forfeit independent representation on that community. Registering with Phil first protects your representation (at no cost) and your leverage.

Are the builder incentives really current?

Yes — that's the point. Incentives change monthly, so Phil verifies them at the source before quoting, with a confirmation date on each. What you get is current and real, not a stale flyer.
