Local Knowledge

Living in the Clear Lake Area, TX

The Clear Lake area is unlike anywhere else in Texas. Built around NASA's Johnson Space Center, this waterfront community carries a unique identity — part coastal town, part aerospace hub, part family suburb. It's where astronauts live, where engineers raise their kids, and where the water is always close.

Clear Creek ISD serves most of the area and consistently ranks among the top school districts in Greater Houston. The proximity to JSC, the University of Houston Clear Lake, and the Bay Area's boating and fishing culture make it a magnet for professionals and outdoor enthusiasts alike.

Phil knows the Clear Lake market well — from the custom waterfront estates in Taylor Lake Village to the solid family homes in Bay Knoll. If you're navigating this market, local knowledge matters more here than almost anywhere else in the Bay Area.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the median home price in the Clear Lake area?

The median home price in the Clear Lake area (77059 ZIP) is approximately $340,000 as of 2026. Waterfront communities like Taylor Lake Village and El Lago command $450K–$1.2M+, while neighborhoods like Bay Knoll and Clear Lake City offer strong value in the $260K–$420K range.

What school district serves the Clear Lake area?

The Clear Lake area is primarily served by Clear Creek ISD, one of the highest-rated school districts in Greater Houston. Clear Lake High School and Clear Creek High School are both A-rated.

What are the best neighborhoods in the Clear Lake area?

Top neighborhoods include Bay Oaks (golf, prestige), Bay Forest (waterfront-adjacent, mature trees), El Lago (waterfront, incorporated), Taylor Lake Village (lakefront, custom homes), and Bay Knoll (family-friendly value).

Is the Clear Lake area a good place to live?

The Clear Lake area offers a rare combination — top schools, water access, NASA culture, and relative affordability compared to similar waterfront markets. It consistently attracts professionals, families, and retirees looking for quality of life south of Houston.

How close is Clear Lake to downtown Houston?

The Clear Lake area is approximately 20–25 miles from downtown Houston, accessible via I-45 South. The drive typically takes 25–35 minutes outside of peak traffic hours.
