The South Shore Harbour Experience

It's not just a neighborhood — it's a lifestyle that took 40 years to build

"South Shore Harbour is the kind of place where Saturday morning means coffee on the dock, a round of golf before noon, and a boat out on Clear Lake by afternoon. You don't vacation here — you live here."

Most master-planned communities in the Houston Bay Area are built around amenities. South Shore Harbour was built around a way of life. The 1,000+ slip marina isn't a marketing feature — it's where residents actually keep their boats and head out to Galveston Bay on weekends. The 27-hole championship golf course isn't background scenery — it's where neighbors meet on Tuesday mornings and Saturday afternoons.

Established in 1983, South Shore Harbour has the one thing that newer communities simply can't manufacture: time. The mature trees, the settled streets, the established social fabric — these are things that take decades to develop. Residents who have been here 20 years stay, and buyers who find it rarely leave.

The range of homes reflects 40 years of growth — from well-maintained 1980s and 90s homes with great bones and character, to newer custom builds on the most coveted waterfront and golf-frontage lots. Whatever your price point, the amenities are the same: marina, golf, resort pools, fitness, tennis, walking trails, and one of the most active social communities in South Houston.

Common Questions

South Shore Harbour — frequently asked questions

What school district is South Shore Harbour in?
South Shore Harbour is fully within Clear Creek ISD (CCISD). Every home in the community feeds into Ferguson Elementary, Bayside Intermediate, and Clear Falls High School. CCISD is consistently rated one of the top school districts in the Houston Bay Area. Always confirm the specific assignment for any address before purchasing.
What are the HOA fees in South Shore Harbour?
South Shore Harbour HOA fees vary by section. Non-gated homes pay approximately $900 per year. Gated sections pay approximately $138 per month in addition to base community fees. These cover the marina, pools, fitness center, tennis courts, parks, trails, and community common areas. Always verify the current fees for the specific section before closing.
Does South Shore Harbour have a marina?
Yes — South Shore Harbour has one of the largest private marinas in the Houston area with over 1,000 boat slips. Residents have direct water access to Clear Lake and from there to Galveston Bay and the Gulf. The marina is a central part of the community lifestyle and one of the top reasons buyers choose South Shore Harbour over other League City neighborhoods.
What is the price range for homes in South Shore Harbour?
Home prices in South Shore Harbour range from approximately $305,000 for smaller established homes to around $1.3 million for premier waterfront and golf-frontage properties — the median sale price is around $425,000. The most active segment is $350K–$550K, where buyers typically find updated homes with partial water or golf views; recent top closes ran $740,000–$850,000, with waterfront listings up to $1.26 million. Waterfront and marina-access properties command significant premiums and represent some of the most desirable real estate in South Houston.
What builders built homes in South Shore Harbour?
South Shore Harbour development began in the early 1980s and involved a mix of builders over four decades: Perry Homes, Emerald Homes, David Weekley Homes, Harbour Classic Builders, and various custom builders. The diversity of builders and construction eras means the community offers genuine variety in style, age, and price point — from 1980s character homes to custom modern estates.
How far is South Shore Harbour from downtown Houston?
South Shore Harbour is approximately 30–35 minutes from downtown Houston via I-45 North. NASA Johnson Space Center is just 10 minutes away, UTMB League City is about 15 minutes, and Kemah Boardwalk is a quick 15-minute drive. For residents with boats, Galveston Bay is accessible directly from the marina.
What restaurants are near South Shore Harbour?
Nearby dining includes Craft 96 Draught House + Kitchen (2575 E League City Pkwy), Lupe Tortilla (5165 TX-96), Ocean Sushi & Grill (3020 Marina Bay Dr), The Spot (2502 Gulf Fwy S), and Kings Biergarten (828 FM 646 Rd, Dickinson). The South Shore Harbour Resort also offers on-site dining within the community, and Kemah Boardwalk's waterfront restaurants are just 15 minutes away.
What grocery stores are near South Shore Harbour?
H-E-B (2755 E League City Pkwy) and Kroger (2750 E League City Pkwy) are both within minutes on East League City Parkway. CVS and Walgreens cover pharmacy and convenience needs. Daily grocery runs typically take under 10 minutes from the neighborhood.
What amenities does South Shore Harbour have?
South Shore Harbour has more built-in amenities than virtually any other neighborhood in the Houston Bay Area: a 1,000+ slip marina with Clear Lake access, a 27-hole championship golf course (designed by 1965 PGA champion Dave Marr and Jay Riviere) and country club, resort-style pools, a full fitness center, tennis and pickleball courts, parks, playgrounds, walking trails, event spaces, and an on-site resort and hotel with dining and spa.
What is it like to live in South Shore Harbour?
Residents consistently describe it as peaceful, active, and like living at a resort. The waterfront lifestyle is real — not a marketing pitch. People boat on weekends, golf on weekday mornings, and use the club for community events year-round. The mature, tree-lined character of a community established in 1983 gives it a warmth and neighborhood identity that newer developments simply haven't had time to build.
