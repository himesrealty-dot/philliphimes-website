Make Your Offer

Know What to Offer
Before You Offer It.

Making an offer without data is a guess. Phil pulls comparable sales, reviews days on market, and reads the seller's situation to help you come in with the right number — competitive, but not overpaying.

  • Comparable Sales Analysis — What have similar homes actually sold for in the last 90 days?
  • Days on Market Read — How long has this listing been sitting? Is the seller motivated?
  • Offer Terms Strategy — Price is just one lever. Closing date, contingencies, and earnest money all matter.
  • Negotiation Positioning — Phil has negotiated hundreds of transactions in this market. He knows what moves sellers.
