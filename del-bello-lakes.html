The Del Bello Lakes Experience

Manvel's lakeside community — scenic water views, newer construction, and standout value per square foot

"Del Bello Lakes buyers are typically getting more home for their money than they'd find in Pearland or League City — newer construction, actual lakefront lots, and the full Manvel lifestyle at a price that leaves room in the budget."

Del Bello Lakes is a newer master-planned community — 448 homesites spread across 216 acres, with more than 40 acres of lakes, five miles of trails, and the Jaxon's Cove rec center anchored by a resort-style pool and splash pad. Building started around 2020, so this is genuinely new construction, not an established neighborhood. The scenic network of lakes woven throughout gives many homes waterfront or water-view settings, and it's one of the reasons buyers who find Del Bello Lakes often stop looking elsewhere.

Homes here are built by Shea Homes, Perry Homes, Ashton Woods, and Westin Homes — a strong lineup of national and regional builders. That means modern open-concept floor plans, higher ceilings, and finishes that don't require immediate updating. For buyers who've toured older inventory in Pearland, League City, or Friendswood and felt the renovation math didn't work, Del Bello Lakes frequently makes more sense.

The Alvin ISD school assignment feeds into E.C. Mason Elementary, Manvel Junior High, and Manvel High School, the district's growing high school serving this part of Brazoria County. One honest note on pricing: recent closed sales are a small sample, so I'd treat the numbers as a current snapshot rather than a hard rule. Combined with the lakeside character and brand-new construction, Del Bello Lakes represents one of the more compelling value propositions in the greater South Houston market.

Common Questions

Del Bello Lakes — frequently asked questions

What school district is Del Bello Lakes in?
Del Bello Lakes is zoned to Alvin Independent School District, with homes feeding into E.C. Mason Elementary, Manvel Junior High, and Manvel High School. School campus assignments are address-specific — always confirm the specific assignment for any address before purchasing.
Are there actual lakefront lots in Del Bello Lakes?
Yes — Del Bello Lakes is built around a network of scenic lakes and ponds, with many homes backing directly to the water. Lakefront lots carry a premium over interior lots. Contact Phil to discuss which lakefront options are currently available and how to evaluate the premium versus the benefit.
What is the price range in Del Bello Lakes?
Home prices in Del Bello Lakes range from approximately $410,000 to $800,000, with a recent median around $595,000. Recent sales are a small sample, so treat these figures as a current snapshot rather than a fixed rule. The most active range is roughly $500K–$650K for newer lake-view and lakefront homes.
How does Del Bello Lakes compare to Rodeo Palms?
Both are strong Manvel communities with Alvin ISD schools, but they have different characters: Rodeo Palms is Manvel's most established community with mature, settled character. Del Bello Lakes offers newer construction and a true lakeside setting. Buyers who prioritize newer homes and water views often choose Del Bello Lakes; buyers who prefer established character often prefer Rodeo Palms. Contact Phil for a side-by-side comparison.
How far is Del Bello Lakes from downtown Houston?
Del Bello Lakes is approximately 35 minutes from downtown Houston via SH 288 or Highway 6 to I-45 — and about 30 minutes to the Texas Medical Center.
What are HOA fees in Del Bello Lakes?
HOA fees in Del Bello Lakes typically range from $800–$1,200 per year, covering community amenities, lake maintenance, and common area upkeep. Always verify the current amount before closing.
