About the District

Why Friendswood ISD Commands a Premium

Friendswood ISD is in a category of its own. With roughly 5,500 students across 6 campuses, it's small by Texas standards — and that's the point. Families who buy in Friendswood ISD are paying a measurable premium for what small means: a community that knows your kids by name, lower student-to-teacher ratios, and a cohesive school culture that's genuinely hard to replicate at scale.

Friendswood High School consistently ranks among the best high schools in Texas — not just in Greater Houston. US News, Niche, and TEA accountability data all confirm what Friendswood families already know: this district delivers.

One important note: not all of Friendswood city is served by Friendswood ISD. Some portions of Friendswood are zoned to Clear Creek ISD (Clear Brook HS) or Pearland ISD. Phil verifies the exact district and campus for any address before you make an offer.

Ask Phil →

Is Friendswood ISD a good school district?

Friendswood ISD is widely regarded as one of the best school districts in Texas. With ~5,500 students, small class sizes, and a cohesive community culture, it consistently earns A ratings from the TEA and top rankings from US News, Niche, and similar publications.

Is all of Friendswood city in Friendswood ISD?

No — this is one of the most common buyer mistakes. Portions of Friendswood city are served by Clear Creek ISD (Clear Brook HS zone) and Pearland ISD. Address-specific verification is essential. Phil confirms the exact district assignment before you make any offer on a Friendswood property.

What is Friendswood High School rated?

Friendswood High School consistently receives A ratings from the Texas Education Agency and ranks in the top 5% of Texas high schools. It is regularly recognized by US News & World Report as a top high school for college preparation and academic achievement.

What is the median home price in Friendswood ISD?

Homes in Friendswood ISD range from around $360K in Falcon Ridge to $1.4M+ in Avalon. The median is approximately $450,000 as of 2026. The district commands a consistent price premium — buying here is a long-term investment in both lifestyle and resale value.

How far is Friendswood from Houston?

Friendswood is approximately 25 miles south of downtown Houston — about a 30-minute commute under normal traffic via I-45 or SH 35. It's also roughly 20–25 minutes from the Texas Medical Center and NASA's Johnson Space Center.
