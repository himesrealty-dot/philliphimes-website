- **Intent matters more than volume.** A "League City realtor" searcher is ready to hire; a "cost of living in League City" searcher is researching. You want both, but transactional terms (Tiers 1тАУ3) convert.
- **You already have pages for most of these** тАФ this maps keywords тЖТ pages so you can see coverage and gaps.
- **No fabricated search volumes here.** To prioritize by real numbers, plug these into the free **Google Keyword Planner** (or Search Console's Performance tab once traffic builds). Treat this as the *target set*; the data tells you the order.
- **The pattern scales.** Where you see `[city]` or `[neighborhood]`, apply it to all 6 cities / 36 neighborhoods.
