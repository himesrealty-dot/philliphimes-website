Why League City?

League City sits at the intersection of everything the Houston area does well: waterfront lifestyle, excellent schools, proximity to major employers, and a strong sense of community.

NASA's Johnson Space Center is 15 minutes away, making League City a hub for aerospace and engineering professionals. The city also has easy access to the Texas Medical Center via I-45 and is one of the fastest-growing cities in the country.

Common Questions About League City

Is League City a good place to live?

Consistently ranked among the best mid-sized cities in Texas. Strong schools, low crime, waterfront access, and proximity to major employers make it ideal for families and professionals alike.

Is League City in a flood zone?

Parts of League City are in flood-prone areas, but most newer communities were built with better drainage infrastructure. I always provide flood map analysis for every property I show. It's one of the most important due-diligence steps in this market.

What is the tax rate in League City?

Property tax rates in League City average approximately 2.1–2.4% depending on the specific community and MUD district. I provide full tax breakdowns for every property.

Clear Lake, TX

Clear Creek ISD Median ~$320K NASA Corridor

Situated along the shores of Clear Lake with NASA's Johnson Space Center as its anchor, this area blends waterfront lifestyle with strong professional community. Clear Creek ISD is well-regarded, and the area offers excellent value relative to its access to the Texas Medical Center, downtown Houston, and major employers. Communities like Bay Forest and El Lago offer mature trees and established character.

Best for: NASA and TMC professionals, waterfront lifestyle buyers, and value-focused families.

Pearland, TX

Pearland ISD Median ~$320K Medical Center Access

One of Houston's fastest-growing cities, Pearland offers tremendous value. It's the closest suburb to the Texas Medical Center and downtown Houston, making it ideal for healthcare and downtown professionals. Pearland ISD continues to improve year over year. Shadow Creek Ranch and Silverlake are popular master-planned communities.

Best for: Medical Center workers and value-focused buyers.

Webster, TX

Clear Creek ISD Median ~$280K Best Value Play
