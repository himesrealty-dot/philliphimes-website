The Bay Knoll Experience

Clear Lake's best-value established neighborhood — CCISD schools at accessible prices

"Bay Knoll gives you the same school district, the same NASA commute, the same Clear Lake lifestyle as the more expensive neighborhoods — at a price point that actually works for most buyers."

Bay Knoll is one of Clear Lake's most compelling value propositions: an established neighborhood with mature character, top-rated Clear Creek ISD schools including Clear Lake High School, and close proximity to NASA JSC — at prices that are meaningfully lower than neighboring Bay Oaks or Bay Forest.

The neighborhood's appeal is straightforward. Buyers who want the Clear Lake lifestyle — the schools, the NASA-corridor employment access, the community — but need to make their budget work can often find it in Bay Knoll. The homes are solid, the streets are established, and the school assignments are the same as in more expensive adjacent neighborhoods.

For aerospace professionals entering the market, Bay Knoll is often the first conversation. A 10-minute commute to NASA JSC, Clear Creek ISD schools, and homes starting around $315K is a combination that's genuinely rare in the Bay Area real estate market.

Common Questions

Bay Knoll — frequently asked questions

What school district is Bay Knoll in?
Bay Knoll is zoned to Clear Creek Independent School District (CCISD), feeding into Clear Lake High School. CCISD is one of the top school districts in the Houston area. Always confirm the specific campus assignment for your address before purchasing.
Is Bay Knoll a good value in Clear Lake?
Yes — Bay Knoll is typically the most accessible entry point into Clear Lake's established CCISD neighborhoods. Buyers get Clear Lake High School, NASA proximity, and the established neighborhood feel at prices that are meaningfully lower than neighboring Bay Oaks or Bay Forest.
What is the price range in Bay Knoll?
Home prices in Bay Knoll range from approximately $315,000 to $450,000 — a tight band that keeps it one of Clear Lake's most accessible established neighborhoods with CCISD schools. The most active range is $375K–$425K for updated move-in ready homes.
How close is Bay Knoll to NASA?
Bay Knoll is approximately 10 minutes from NASA Johnson Space Center — making it excellent for aerospace professionals who want affordable homeownership with a short commute.
What are the HOA fees in Bay Knoll?
Bay Knoll HOA fees typically range from $400–$700 per year. Always verify the current amount before closing.
How far is Bay Knoll from downtown Houston?
Bay Knoll is approximately 30 minutes from downtown Houston via I-45 — a reasonable commute for professionals who prioritize the Clear Lake lifestyle, CCISD schools, and NASA proximity over a shorter Houston commute.
