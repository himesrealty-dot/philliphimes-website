The Mar Bella Experience

League City's most striking neighborhood — and it delivers on every detail

"You drive through and the palm trees hit you first. Then the lakes. Then you realize — this doesn't feel like any other neighborhood in League City. It feels like somewhere you chose to live on purpose."

Most neighborhoods look like neighborhoods. Mar Bella looks like a destination. The palm-lined boulevards, Mediterranean-inspired architecture, and 40 acres of interconnected lakes create a visual identity that's genuinely unlike anything else in League City — or the Houston Bay Area. It wasn't an accident; it was designed that way.

But aesthetics are just the entry point. What keeps people in Mar Bella is the livability: the resort pool and splash pad that actually get used, the trails that wind past the water, the community events, and the kind of neighbors who chose this neighborhood for the same reasons you did. It's a community for people who care about where they live.

CCISD schools, easy access to I-45, and a location that puts NASA and UTMB within 15 minutes makes Mar Bella one of League City's most well-rounded choices — for families, professionals, and anyone who wants the best of both worlds.

Common Questions

Mar Bella — frequently asked questions

What school district is Mar Bella in?
Mar Bella is fully within Clear Creek ISD (CCISD). The community feeds into Clear Springs High School, one of the district's highest-ranked campuses. Always confirm the specific school assignment for any address before purchasing — assignments can vary by street.
What are the HOA fees in Mar Bella?
Mar Bella HOA fees are approximately $1,200 per year. This is higher than some neighboring communities and reflects the premium amenities — 40 acres of lakes, the resort pool and splash pad, trail maintenance, and the Mediterranean streetscape. Always verify the current amount with the HOA before closing.
What builders built homes in Mar Bella?
Mar Bella was primarily developed by Perry Homes, NewMark Homes, and David Weekley Homes. Each brought distinct floor plans and finishes inspired by the Mediterranean theme. Perry and NewMark handled most of the production homes; David Weekley contributed larger, more custom-oriented homes in select sections.
How far is Mar Bella from downtown Houston?
Mar Bella is approximately 25–30 minutes from downtown Houston via I-45 North. Its southeastern League City location puts the freeway just a short drive away, making the commute efficient without placing residents directly on the corridor.
What is the price range for homes in Mar Bella?
Home prices in Mar Bella range from approximately $335,000 at the entry level to $800,000+ for premium lakefront homes — the median sale price is around $422,000. The most active price band is $340K–$530K, where buyers find well-appointed homes with upgraded finishes and often lake or greenbelt views; the top recent close was $815,000. Lakefront homes on the 40-acre lake system command a significant premium.
What amenities does Mar Bella have?
Mar Bella features 40 acres of scenic lakes, a resort-style swimming pool and splash pad, miles of walking and biking trails, fishing areas, playgrounds, and the most architecturally distinctive streetscape in League City — complete with palm-lined boulevards and Mediterranean-inspired entry features.
What makes Mar Bella different from other League City neighborhoods?
Mar Bella is simply unlike anything else in League City. The Mediterranean architecture, palm-lined boulevards, and 40-acre lake system create a visual identity that stands apart from every other master-planned community in the area. It draws buyers who want their neighborhood to feel like a deliberate choice — not just a zip code.
What is the closest hospital to Mar Bella?
The closest major hospital is UTMB Health League City Campus at 2240 Gulf Fwy S — approximately 10 minutes from Mar Bella. It is a full University of Texas Medical Branch facility with a Level II trauma center and 24-hour emergency services. Kelsey-Seybold Clinic (3625 E League City Pkwy) handles multi-specialty outpatient care nearby.
