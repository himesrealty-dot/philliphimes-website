Local Knowledge

Living in League City, TX

League City is one of the fastest-growing cities in Texas — and for good reason. Located just 25 miles southeast of downtown Houston, it sits at the intersection of opportunity: top-rated schools, master-planned communities, easy freeway access, and proximity to both NASA's Johnson Space Center and the Texas Medical Center.

The city offers an exceptional quality of life across every price point — from first-time buyer communities like Victory Lakes to luxury waterfront estates in South Shore Harbour. Whether you're a growing family, an empty nester ready to downsize, or a relocating professional, League City has a neighborhood built for your life stage.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the median home price in League City, TX?

The median home price in League City is approximately $375,000 as of 2026. Prices range from the high $200s in communities like Victory Lakes to over $1M in waterfront neighborhoods like South Shore Harbour.

What school district is League City in?

Most of League City falls within Clear Creek ISD, one of the highest-rated school districts in Greater Houston. Some western areas are served by Dickinson ISD. Clear Creek ISD schools are consistently recognized for academic performance and extracurricular programs.

What are the best neighborhoods in League City?

The most sought-after neighborhoods include Tuscan Lakes, South Shore Harbour, Mar Bella, Brittany Lakes, Clear Creek Village, and Victory Lakes — each offering a different lifestyle, price range, and feel.

How long does it take to sell a home in League City?

Well-priced homes in League City typically sell in 21–35 days. Homes in top communities that are properly staged and priced often receive multiple offers within the first two weeks.

Is League City a good place to buy real estate?

Yes — League City's population growth, strong school system, and diversified economy (NASA, Medical Center proximity, port industry) make it one of the most stable and consistently appreciating markets in the Greater Houston area.
