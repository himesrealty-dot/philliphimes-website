The Taylor Lake Village Experience

Waterfront prestige with top CCISD schools — the Clear Lake area's most distinctive community

"Taylor Lake Village buyers aren't just buying a house — they're buying into one of the Bay Area's most coveted small cities, with its own character, waterfront access, and top-rated schools."

Taylor Lake Village is unlike any other community in the Clear Lake area. It's an actual incorporated city with its own government and identity — not just a subdivision. The community wraps around Taylor Lake and offers a genuine waterfront lifestyle that other Clear Lake neighborhoods can only approximate.

The appeal is layered: top-rated Clear Creek ISD schools including Clear Lake High School, about 10 minutes to NASA JSC for aerospace professionals, and a community that has preserved its small-city character through decades of growth in the surrounding area. Homes range from well-maintained mid-century estates to contemporary renovations, with waterfront lots commanding a meaningful premium.

For buyers who want the Clear Lake package — NASA access, CCISD schools, waterfront lifestyle — at the upper-mid range of the market, Taylor Lake Village is consistently one of the area's most compelling options.

Common Questions

Taylor Lake Village — frequently asked questions

Is Taylor Lake Village its own city?
Yes — Taylor Lake Village is an incorporated municipality in Harris County with its own city government. It's not just a subdivision — it's a real city with its own identity within the Clear Lake area, which contributes to its distinctive character and desirability.
What school district is Taylor Lake Village in?
Taylor Lake Village is zoned to Clear Creek Independent School District (CCISD), feeding into Clear Lake High School. CCISD is one of the top districts in the Houston metro. Always confirm the specific campus for your address before purchasing.
What is the price range in Taylor Lake Village?
Home prices in Taylor Lake Village range from approximately $315,000 to $750,000+ — and it's really a two-tier market. Established 1960s sections like Timber Cove and Clear Lake Forest typically close between $315K and $440K, while newer lakefront construction at Taylor Lake Place trades from $590K to $750K+. Recent sales are a small sample, so confirm current comps for any specific property.
Does Taylor Lake Village have waterfront homes?
Yes — Taylor Lake Village has genuine waterfront properties along Taylor Lake, including homes with private docks and direct water access. These represent the premium tier of the community's market.
How far is Taylor Lake Village from NASA?
Taylor Lake Village is approximately 10 minutes from NASA Johnson Space Center — making it one of the most convenient waterfront communities for aerospace professionals in the entire Bay Area.
How far is Taylor Lake Village from downtown Houston?
Taylor Lake Village is approximately 30 minutes from downtown Houston via I-45 — consistent with other Clear Lake area communities. Most residents prioritize NASA access, CCISD schools, and the waterfront lifestyle over a shorter Houston commute.
